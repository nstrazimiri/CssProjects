Rita Ora is one of the most famous Albanian singer, along with Dua Lipa, Bebe Rexha, Era Istrefi etc.

She was born on 26 November 1990 in Pristina, Kosovo, but 1 year later her family moved to England. She rose to prominence in February 2012 when she featured on DJ Fresh's single "Hot Right Now", which reached number one in the UK. Her debut studio album, Ora, released in August 2012, debuted at number one in the United Kingdom. The album contained the UK number-one singles "R.I.P." and "How We Do (Party)". Ora was the artist with the most number-one singles on the UK Singles Chart in 2012, with three consecutive singles reaching the top position.
